Logistics

  • Hotels: We're working with our friendly planner to put together a hotel block with actual reservations, and you can trust that this is upcoming. Note that it'll probably be something affordable like a Holiday Inn Express. For something different, you're free to book things yourself - we recommend the Omni for upscale shiny rooms or various Albemarle County campgrounds if you'd prefer a tent.
  • Transport: Charlottesville is quite accessible for many of the guests, about a 5-6 hour drive from either Pittsburgh or Philadelphia, and a quick drive or train ride from Washington. If you're coming from further away, like Minneapolis, the CHO Airport has reasonably convenient connections through American, Delta, and United.

  • More to Come! We have plenty more to tell you and this page will be updated when more complete invites go out. If you have ideas for what information guests would like, let us know and we'll think it through.
