Thoughts on Ambience!

Decoration

Brittany really loves things that take up vertical space - balloons, paper, streamers, lights, and so on.

Use decorations to create zones - the porch zone, the woods zone, the bar zone, the upstairs zone.

Encourage people to move from zone to zone because the space itself is inherently interesting, not just because there might be food there.

Encourage movement and socializing by the guests - a scavenger hunt might come into play here.

Favors

Packets of wildflower seeds - with instructions about how they relate to monarchs.
